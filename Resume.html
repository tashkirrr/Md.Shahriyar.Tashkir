Some people choose between being a designer or a developer. I never understood why I had to pick one. I'm Mohammad Shahriyar Tashkir, a Computer Science student at Daffodil International University. I design brands, write about machine learning, and lead digital strategy for a youth environmental organization. Currently, I work as the Director of IT and Graphics at Youth For a Green Earth. I manage everything visual, from event campaigns to social media identity. Previously, I won the Graphics Design Championship at Adamjee Cantonment College's TechCon 2024 and served as the General Secretary of my school's IT Club. On the tech side, I am really interested in the connection between Machine Learning and Cryptography. I focus on how Homomorphic Encryption and Federated Learning can help us create smarter models without using raw data. I share these ideas on Medium because I believe complex topics need clear explanations. In addition to all this, I have represented Bangladesh as a National Ambassador for the ICT Olympiad. I volunteered at the U.S. Embassy’s Mini College Fair and became a Divisional Champion in ICT while I was still in school. I think coding and designing are just different ways to solve problems elegantly.
